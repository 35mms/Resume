I am a California-based film director primarily exploring topics such as youth and development, as well as personhood and human purpose with the universe.

Growing up in a small suburban town in Southern California, I was heavily restricted in regards to exploring my creative ideas. My creativity was often stagnant, purely inhibited by my sheltered home life and limited connections to those around me. When I was a child, I spent most of my free-time rather isolated and focusing most of my energy into drawing and painting- at first traditionally, but then later moving to the digital realm- instead of building relationships with others. This commitment not only brought me newfound insight on my place in this world and my relationships with others, but it also allowed me to express my thoughts and ideas in an unconventional way.

As several years passed, I found myself drawn by capturing life's precious moments, both in the form of photography and film. Since exploring my passion, I've been commissioned by multiple people to take their portraits professionally in high school. Outside of these small projects, most of my work is composed of primarily my own original ideas as I attempt to incorporate my own personal experiences and thoughts.
